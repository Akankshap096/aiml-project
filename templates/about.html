ABOUT THE PROJECT

PlantCare AI

An advanced plant disease detection system built with Transfer Learning and MobileNetV2, achieving 96.6% validation accuracy on 87,000+ leaf images.

OVERVIEW

What is PlantCare AI?

PlantCare AI is an AI-powered plant pathology tool that uses deep learning to identify diseases from leaf images. It leverages MobileNetV2 — a lightweight yet powerful convolutional neural network — trained via Transfer Learning on the New Plant Diseases Dataset.

The system classifies leaves into 38 categories across 14 plant types, distinguishing between healthy plants and 37 distinct diseases — providing farmers, researchers, and gardeners with instant, accurate diagnoses.

TRAINING DETAILS

Model Training Pipeline

📦

Dataset

  • 87,000+ augmented JPEG images
  • 38 classes · 14 crop types
  • 256×256px → resized to 224×224
  • 80% train / 20% validation split
⚙️

Compilation

  • Optimizer: Adam (lr = 1e-4)
  • Loss: Categorical Crossentropy
  • Metric: Accuracy
  • Batch size: 32
🛡️

Callbacks

  • ModelCheckpoint — saves best model
  • ReduceLROnPlateau — lr ×0.5 after 3 epochs
  • EarlyStopping — stops after 6 epochs no gain
📈

Results

  • Train accuracy: ~95%+
  • Val accuracy: 96.6% (peak)
  • No significant overfitting
  • Converged in ~5 epochs
TECHNOLOGY

Why MobileNetV2?

🪶

Lightweight

Uses depthwise separable convolutions — far fewer parameters than VGG or ResNet while maintaining competitive accuracy.

🔁

Inverted Residuals

Linear bottleneck layers with inverted residual connections allow efficient feature reuse without gradient degradation.

🏋️

Pre-trained Weights

Trained on ImageNet with 1,000 classes — enables powerful feature extraction without training from scratch.

📱

Edge-Ready

Optimized for mobile and embedded systems — ideal for future deployment in farm IoT devices and mobile apps.
